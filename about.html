Dirty Electro is an electronic music forum affiliated with the H-Estrella Team. The website was launched in Amsterdam, the Netherlands on June 6, 2022, and the closed beta began on June 24. On June 25, THE U.S. DEPARTMENT of STATE directly invested in the website and may become the largest shareholder of Dirty Electro USA. On the same day, Chinese video website Bilibili.com and H-Estrella Team signed 21 cooperation projects and became H-Estrella Team. The second largest partner of Estrella Team.
