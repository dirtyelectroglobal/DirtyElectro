About

Unity Innovation Independence

H-ESTRELLA TEAM is an independent game music studio founded by ChengHsüan Wu. In May 2022, H-ESTRELLA GAMING TEAM and H-ESTRELLA MUSIC STUDIO merged to form H-ESTRELLA TEAM in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. The purpose of H-ESTRELLA TEAM is to bring a better experience to users and add vitality to the Internet. TEAM will launch more new products in the future and work hand in hand with users.
